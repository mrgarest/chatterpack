# Privacy Policy for Chatterpack

**Created: April 1, 2026**

The [Chatterpack](https://github.com/mrgarest/chatterpack) extension for Twitch does not collect or transmit any user data.

All of the extension's features run locally in your browser.

## Changes to the Privacy Policy

All updates and changes to this [Privacy Policy](https://github.com/mrgarest/chatterpack/blob/main/PRIVACY-POLICY.md) will be posted on this page and will take effect upon publication.
